About

This is a web based tool, to automatically parse and process results of Super Animal Royale tournaments, usually called Scrims. This tool was written by me (Trikami) (GitHub, Twitter, Twitch) wanting to do more Scrims in SAR, but I was too lazy to punch these numbers into Excel by hand every time.

The project is open to contributions on GitHub.
